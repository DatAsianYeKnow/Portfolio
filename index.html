About Me

I'm an ambitious scholar who wants to impact the world significantly, which requires a change. I want to innovate renewable energy technology that's more efficient and accessible because I'm adamant about sustainability and believe achieving this requires renewable, clean energy and technology. Initially, I never understood why I wanted to change the world for the better, but it was due to the guilt and despair of knowing that people were suffering when I could do something about it. I admit I'm naive, but what's naive with desiring to aid others and becoming a better individual? I constantly strive for improvement, physically or mentally, because I loathe the idea of not being the best me. I remember fading into the background during most of my life, but I've realized that when given a chance to refine my qualities, I will exceed everyone's expectations, including mine.
